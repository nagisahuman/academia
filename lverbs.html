Present

  • Is happening now.
  • The best way to identify this is by knowing what it is.
  • It can also be identified through knowing that it is not imperfect or perfect.
  • Often follows these endings:
Singular Plural
I/we o mus
You s tis
He/She/It/They t nt

Examples:

  • ambulo ‐ I am walking
  • amat ‐ He loves

Imperfect

  • Was happening
  • Can be identified by ‐ba‐
  • ambulabam ‐ I was walking
