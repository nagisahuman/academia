Washington

  • Government & Finances
    • Department of State, Department of Treasury, Department of War, and the Office of the Attorney General are created to manage different parts of the government.
    • Tariff of 1789 is passed to tax imports.
    • Congress accepts all Continental Congress debts.
    • The first Bank of the U. S. is established in 1791.
    • The Congress taxes manufacture of whiskey. The Whiskey Rebellion happens, in which farmers attack tax collectors and generally rebel. In response, Washington sends the army to stop the rebellion.
  • Federalists
    • Led by Hamilton.
    • Strong federal government.
  • Democratic‐Republicans Modern‐day Democrats
    • Led by Jefferson.
    • Favored agrarianism ‐ an agricultural government.
    • Wanted stronger state government.
