American Nationalism

  • Economy
    • Second Bank of the U. S. is created, leading to national currency.
    • The Tariff of 1816 is created to help local businesses through taxing imports.
  • Judiciary
    • Martin v. Hunter's Lessee shows Supreme Court is supreme.
    • McCulloch v. Maryland shows that the state government cannot interfere with the federal government.
    • Gibbons v. Ogden shows that the federal government is supreme in regards to interstate transportation
  • Diplomacy
    • Andrew Jackson invades Florida. This causes the Adams‐Onis Treaty, which gives Florida to the U. S.
    • The Monroe Doctrine is created to say that the Americas are not to be colonized, and that any attempt to do so will be seen as a direct attack on the United States.
