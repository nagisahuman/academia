DNA Replication

Esentially, the DNA is separated into two strands. These strands are templates for the new strands of DNA. Enzymes then fill in the bases, creating two strands of DNA.
Enzymes:

  • Helicase separates the DNA strands
  • Primase makes RNA primer
  • Polymerase binds to the primer. Also fills in the new DNA strands
  • Ligase joins the fragments together to finish replicating DNA
