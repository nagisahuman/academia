Macroevolution is evolution over a very large period of time. Examples of macroevolution are:

  • Extinction ‐ a species no longer existing.
  • Adaptive radiation ‐ a single species evolves into multiple species.
  • Convergent evolution ‐ different species begin to resemble each other.
  • Coevolution ‐ species evolve in response to each other.
  • Gradualism ‐ the idea that species change gradually
  • Punctuated equilibrium ‐ the idea that stable periods are followed by periods of rapid evolution.
