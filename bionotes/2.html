Matter

Atom
the smallest unit.
Nucleus
center of an atom. Made up of protons and neutrons.
Protons
positively charged particles.
Neutrons
particles with no charge.
Electrons
negatively charged particles.
Element
a substance with only one type of atom.
Ions
have a different number of electrons.
Isotopes
atoms with a different number of neutrons than protons.
Ionic bonds
a bond in which electrons are transferred between atoms.
Covalent bond
a bond in which electrons are shared between atoms.
van der Waals forces
the slight attraction between molecules.
hydrogen bond
a bond between hydrogen molecules
glycosidic bond
a bond between carbohydrates
peptide bond
a bond between amino acids
sulfur bond
a bond between proteins

Water

  • Polarity
    • Water molecules have an uneven distribution of electrons
    • Oxygen is slightly negative.
    • Hydrogen is slightly positive.
  • Bonds
    • Cohesion ‐ the attraction between molecules of the same substance.
    • Adhesion ‐ the attraction between molecules of different substances.

Combinations of Stuff

  • Mixtures are made up of two or more things that are mixed physically. They are not mixed chemically.
  • In a solution, something is dissolved and equally distributed throughout something else.
    • The thing being dissolved is the solute.
    • The thing the solute is being dissolved into is the solvent.
    • Water is the universal solvent.
  • Suspensions are made up of water and an undissolved material.

pH scale

  • 14 ‐ highly basic
  • 12.5 ‐ bleach
  • 7.5 ‐ human blood
  • 7 ‐ neutral
  • 2.5 ‐ Lemon Juice
  • 1.5 ‐ stomach acid
  • 0 ‐ highly acidic

A buffer is a weak acid or base. Buffers react with strong acids and bases to prevent sudden pH changes.

Enzymes

chemical reaction
a process which changes chemicals into different chemicals.
reactant
the chemical or chemicals going into a chemical reaction
product
the result of a chemical reaction
activation energy
energy needed to activate a reaction
catalyst
a substance which speeds up the rate of a chemical reaction. Without these
many processes necessary for life would take much longer.
enzyme
a protein that acts as a biological catalyst. These lower the amount of activation energy needed.
substrates
the reactants in chemical reactions that used enzymes.
