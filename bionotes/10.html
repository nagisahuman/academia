Mitosis

  • Interphase
    • G0 ‐ cell rest? Unknown.
    • G1 ‐ organelle duplication
    • S ‐ DNA replication
    • G2 ‐ finish replication. Preparation for mitosis.
  • Cell division or M Phase or mitosis
    • Prophase ‐ chromosomes become visible. Centrioles move to poles and form spindle. Nucleus disappears.
    • Metaphase ‐ chromosomes move to center and connect to spindle.
    • Anaphase ‐ chromosomes are separated and pulled towards poles.
    • Telophase ‐ chromosomes move to poles. Nuclear envelopes reform.
    • Cytokinesis ‐ physical division into two daughter cells.
