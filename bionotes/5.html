Limiting factors

A limiting factor is a factor that causes the population to decrease. These factors can be density dependent or density independent.

Density dependent factors include:

  • competition
  • predation
  • parisitism
  • disease

Density independent factors include:

  • sudden, odd weather
  • natural disasters
  • human interactions with the environment
  • seasonal changes
